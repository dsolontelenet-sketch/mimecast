How to report urgent customer issues directly via the Urgent Customer Alert channel — quickly and correctly, every time.

What This Guide Covers
  • 01 A copy-ready alert message to paste into the Urgent Customer Alert channel
  • 02 How to highlight 【URGENT】 in red inside the editor
  • 03 Annotated screenshots of each editor step
!

Do NOT send a 1st response to Mimecast first

As soon as you identify an urgent issue, go straight to "Urgent Customer Alert" and report it there. Skipping the first-response step means faster escalation and faster fixes for the customer.

Step 01

Copy & Paste the Alert Message

alert-template.txt
【URGENT】 Hi Everyone, We would like to share an email from Swati regarding the Miscategorization Bug - Fitness Sites Tagged as Illegal Activities in Netstar URL categorization. Please see the details below. Email Title Re: Miscategorization Bug - Fitness Sites Tagged as Illegal Activities in Netstar URL categorization From sswati@mimecast.com Date 05/19/2026 5:50 PM Overview Swati confirmed that the new config files now return the correct category, i.e. FITNESS, for the fitness domains they shared. It appears the issue was caused by outdated category files. They will also update all their servers.
✓ Copied to clipboard — paste it into Urgent Customer Alert now

After pasting, still highlight 【URGENT】 in red — see Step 02 below.

Step 02

Highlight 【URGENT】 in Red

After pasting, follow these 4 steps inside the editor to make 【URGENT】 instantly visible.

  1. 01

    Open the text editor

    Click the pencil ✏️ icon at the bottom-right corner of the editor to enter edit mode.

  2. 02

    Select the 【URGENT】 text

    Click and drag to highlight only the 【URGENT】 tag at the very start of the message.

  3. 03

    Click the Highlight icon

    In the formatting toolbar, find the Highlight / Background Colour icon (marker or paint bucket).

  4. 04

    Choose Red from the colour palette

    Select Red from the picker. The 【URGENT】 tag will immediately get a red background — making it instantly noticeable.
